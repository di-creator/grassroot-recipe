Local Ghanaian Dish - Banku & Tilapia

Preparation Process

Banku and Tilapia is a native food in Ghana. This food enjoyed by every tribe in Ghana. The preparation process is simple and easy for everyone.

No special skills is required.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Tilapia
  • Ginger and Garlic
  • Corn dough
  • Cassava dough
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Season your tilipia with grinded ginger and garlic
  2. Marinade for 30mins
  3. Add your corn and cassava dough together in a pot
  4. Put your mixture on fire and stir till hard
  5. Take firsh out of marinde bowl and grill
